What is dyno test?

Dyno test are rollers on which you put your car and it measure your car power, Nm, and accelereation.
With result you get graphs with all necessary informations.It does not matter if you have
a car which is 2WD or 4WD, is it RWD or FWD it will be correctly measured anyway.
If you ask you self why to dyno test your car, we will tell you down below.

Car performance

You get graph with current car power and torque

Acceleration

It display time needed for acceleration of yours car

Free install

Install of each upgrade is free

Free advice

You will get advice what should be best to upgrade on your car

All info on phone

Graphs with performance info will be send to your phone

Save your nerves

Save yourselves and your nerves from boring driving
